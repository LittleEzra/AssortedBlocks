## Added:
- Corpse Flower
    - Dug up by Sniffers in Crimson and Warped Nylium
    - Makes fly particles
    - Scares nearby mobs away
- Fly Lure
    - Crafted with a Corpse Flower
    - Makes fly particles (in a larger radius than the Corpse Flower)
    - Doesn't scare mobs
- Honeydew Fruit
    - Found as a random drop alongside Raw Gold in Gold Ore
    - Must be planted on any stone-like block (including Rocky Dirt)
