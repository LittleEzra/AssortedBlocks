## Added:
- Leeching Soil
    - Crafted with 2 Soul Sand or Soul Soil, two Dirt, and two Bones
    - Will change certain blocks above itself
    - Can be waxed like copper to stop this
    - This feature is data-driven using a data map with the id "artistry:leechables" for blocks
    - Has JEI integration to show all leechables
- Rotten Trees
    - The sapling is obtained by placing any non-rotten sapling on Leeching Soil
    - Can be grown like any other tree
- Marigold
    - Obtained by placing a small flower on Leeching Soil
    - Gives Slow Falling when used for Suspicious Stew
- Corpse Flower
    - Seed dug up by Sniffers in Crimson and Warped Nylium
    - Makes fly particles
    - Scares nearby mobs away
    - ! UNOBTAINABLE !
- Fly Lure
    - Crafted with a Corpse Flower
    - Makes fly particles (in a larger radius than the Corpse Flower)
    - Doesn't scare mobs
    - ! UNOBTAINABLE !
- Glowing Mushroom
    - Obtained by growing a mushroom on an Amethyst Block
- Carving Knife
    - Crafted like an Iron Sword but diagonal
    - Can be used like shears on pumpkins (including Carved Pumpkins) to carve them into one of 7 variants (including vanilla Carved Pumpkins)
- Tall Candles
    - Available in all the colors that normal candles have
    - Can be stacked to make even taller candles
- Urn
    - Acts as a single-item storage like a decorated pot
